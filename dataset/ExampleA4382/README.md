The following files correspond to the plate number A4382 by the Cerro Tololo Inter-American Observatory (CTIO) on 1976-02-16. There are two observations inside this plate: (1) the star Zet Vir (HD 125162) and (2) the star HD 148937 with coordinates RA: 16:33:24.5 DEC: -48:07:50.0.

From the plate, we have extracted the following spectra:
- `CE_` are the iron lamp spectra without wavelength calibration.
- `WEC_` are the iron lamp spectra with wavelength calibration.
  - the `_CUT` suffix indicates that the spectra were cut to the region of interest.
- `E_` are the science spectra without wavelength calibration.
- `WE_` are the science spectra with wavelength calibration.
  - the `_NORM` suffix indicates that the spectra were cut to the region of interest and normalized.
